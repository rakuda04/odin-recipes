Mac&Cheese

Description

I've always loved mac and cheese, but it wasn't until I was in college that I really learned how to make it properly. My roommates and I were always looking for ways to save money, and we found that we could make a really delicious and satisfying mac and cheese for a fraction of the cost of buying it from a restaurant.

We started by experimenting with different recipes, and we eventually settled on one that we all loved. The key to our mac and cheese is using a combination of cheeses, including cheddar, Gruyère, and Parmesan. We also add a touch of nutmeg and cayenne pepper for some extra flavor.

The first time I made mac and cheese from scratch, I was a little nervous. I didn't want to mess it up, but I was determined to give it a try. I followed the recipe carefully, and I was so happy with the results. The mac and cheese was creamy, cheesy, and flavorful. My roommates all loved it, and I was so proud of myself for making it. Since then, I've made mac and cheese many times, and I've always gotten rave reviews. It's become a staple in my repertoire, and I love sharing it with friends and family.

Ingredients

  • 1 package elbow macaroni
  • 1 rotisserie-roasted chicken
  • 6 tablespoons butter
  • 6 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 3 cups milk
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • ½ cup crumbled gorgonzola cheese
